ABOUT US

In 2023 Ancient sect was just an idea of a student and was made due to his requirements for the prelim exam. its mission is to give more power to consumers and business owners when they bank, pay and invest.

Ancient Sect was born out of an idea to simplify accepting payments online. in 2022 before Ancient Sect was created, setting up payment provider for a website was difficult, time-consuming and often thousands of pesos before even accepting the first payment or testing out the checkout.

Now, everyone can set up an Ancient Sect account to accept payment at no initial cost in minutes using beautiful pre-made checkout elements.
